50  Returns the transformation matrix (4 X 4) based on Denavit-Hartenberg standard. (Reference 1, pages 36 to 40)
51  '''
52  sa = math.sin(alpha)

56 
57  TM = numpy.array( [ [ct , -ca*st, sa*st, a*ct ],
